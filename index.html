Why Dental Minion?

Simply: Fast and Accurate

The Dental Minion program uses a convolutional neural network, a class of deep neural networks. The machine learning model process the dental radiograph digital image and provides the dentist with deep expertise in milliseconds.

Our Machine Learning model is trained with 4000+ dental radiographs and has an average accuracy of 95% in caries detection with fast estimation result in 160 milliseconds (average). Dental Minionensures that you can diagnose your patients quickly and accurately. In addition to significantly improving patient interaction, Dental Minion increases your revenue by increasing patient throughput.
